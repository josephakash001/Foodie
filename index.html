Food Menu

Dosa

Dosa is a thin, crispy South Indian pancake made from fermented rice and urad dal batter, commonly served with sambar and chutney. It is known for its light texture and versatile flavor.

Price: ₹ 40

Idly

Idly is a traditional South Indian steamed rice cake made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal, presenting a soft, spongy texture. Typically served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Price: ₹ 10

South Indian Meals

A traditional South Indian meal, often served on a banana leaf, includes staples like rice, sambar (spiced lentil soup), rasam (tamarind-based soup)

Price: ₹ 150

Biriyani

Biriyani is a flavorful and aromatic South Asian dish made with fragrant basmati rice, meat (such as chicken, mutton, or beef), or vegetables, and a blend of spices.

Price: ₹ 250
